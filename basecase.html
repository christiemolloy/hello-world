Basecase was created during Boston’s Girl’s Who Code Hackathon that I participated in with a team of three. The prompt was to develop something that encourages females to work in the tech industry. Our first experience with tech was with building our first website, but we discussed some of the challenges we faced with understanding how to use a text editor, what GitHub was, how to use the terminal, and buying a domain name. In our team we designed and developed Basecase, an iOS app that guides users through creating a website or building an iOS app, step by step. It provides progress updates, sets goals, and unpacks terminology, to make developing a fun and successful experience.
