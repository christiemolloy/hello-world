ISOLATION PROJECT

Thanks for stopping by. My current project in Design Class involves the use of a projector to make a statement about the difference between the feeling of loneliness and just being alone.

To do this, I am collecting real stories from people at Boston University to make my installation authentic. These stories will be projected on people and photographed. If you feel as though your story or experience would make a positive impact then please share it! Your input will be entirely anonymous when you fill in the required fields below.

You can submit anything from a sentence to a few paragraphs. It could relate to an experience one day or a general feeling of isolation. Don’t feel scared to post your thoughts – because you are not alone.
