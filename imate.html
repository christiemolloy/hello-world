iMate Meet the future. Meet your new mate: iMate

The process of waking up in the morning is utterly different for everyone. However, the iPhone is currently limited to the same restricted wake-up process for everyone. The alarm sounds, they check the time and their notifications, they possibly read the news or open Facebook, Instagram or Snapchat to see what they missed overnight. They could be in a new city so they plan what to do that day, or maybe they attempt a new workout. Basically, the iPhone controls millions of users mornings, but the time has come for customization. Meet the future. Meet your new mate: iMate. Wake up in the morning with a friend, an instructor, or a guide and her name isn’t Siri; Siri is down-right annoying. Be excited to wake up in the morning on the right path with your new friend. Once the alarm sounds, rather than sliding to unlock, users can slide to start their morning. They will be greeted with their favourite quote, the weather in their city today, and a list of their notifications from overnight. Following from that, it is all fully customizable with over 200 different options to include in the morning depending on the amount of time they have in the morning and their personality, interests and goals.

Work Schedule: “Work begins in “45 minutes. According to current traffic conditions, you would need to leave in 30 minutes to make it by 9am. Would you like me to order your uber?” “I could also pre-order your flat-white at the Starbucks across the road from work?”

Global + Local News: “Overnight, CNN reported on some breaking news: Trump is now the president of the United States, Greece is no longer in financial debt and there was a shooting in Alabama”.

Local Events: “In your area today there are several events taking place, tap to see them”.

Workouts: Your workout today will be a 5 mile run. Lets aim to complete it in 30 minutes, take me on your run and I will help you pace it! I also have your workout playlist ready! Lets start by doing 5 minutes of core exercises before you run.

Meditation: Okay Anna, we are going to do 5 minutes of meditation. Start by inhaling through your nose and holding for 5 seconds before a long exhale…

Brain Training: For younger: Okay Brian, today we are learning the multiplication table for 9. What is 9x1? *Enters 9*. Correct! What is 9x2? *Enters 18*. Correct! For older: Today we are going to test your memory. Once you hear the sounds, enter the order that you heard them in.

Groupons: I had a look at some of the best groupons out today that I think you would like. You can get 75% off of a spray tan, you can watch a new-release movie for $5, or you receive 25% off at Brandy and Melville. Tap to see more…

Break-Up: It’s been 14 days since the break-up. You’re doing so well! Remember everyday is going to get easier, and it’s all about making small steps. I checked out several dating apps, and it looks like there are some very cute males around the city that have similar interests to you. Tonight there is an event for singles downtown! Tap to see more…
