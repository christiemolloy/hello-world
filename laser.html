Is Instagram really censored? 2. Laser Cutter + Social Media

I believe one of the major issues with social media is its transparancy. Instagram claims to censor images, yet it is not effective whatsoever. The amount of nudity and inappropriate imagery that exists on the application is atrocious, especially when young kids are one of the highest user demographics, and high-end businesses use the application to market their brand. So, I tailored my project around this concept and use the laser cutter to emphasize the transparency of Instagram.
