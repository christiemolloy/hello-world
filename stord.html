Stor'd Never have to take a printed receipt again.

The idea first came to me when I was shopping with my mom and as I was taking the grocery bags at the checkout I was handed my receipt. I subsequently scrunched up the receipt and threw it in the trash. It was at that moment I posed the question “why do paper receipts still exist?” They are useless, wasteful and inefficient, and currently, there is not one application that makes receipts interactive. Yes you can view receipts online, but can you actually USE your receipt. The ideal user would be frequent grocery shopper (mom), however the application would be used by anyone who shops and cares about their budget.At the checkout, once the user has swiped their card, they can open their Stor’d app and view their most recent receipt. They can click on any item on the receipt taking them to their weekly, monthly and yearly spending on that item group. It will show them where they could have bought it cheaper and allow them to add it to their future shopping list in a certain amount of days. This automatically creates the second function of the app for the user: a virtual shopping list. Stor’d will calculate, based on all the items in the shopping list, where to shop based on the lowest total price of all items. Stor’d not only benefits the user by saving them money, but it also benefits stores. They are able to log into the application and view accumulated shopping lists based on the region they select. For example, a store manager can log in to Stor’d and view the accumulated shopping list for all users in the Boston area ranked in order of the most popular items. Currently, there is no other way for stores to determine consumers’ future purchases, other than basing it off of past trends. Stores will be able to administer specials and savings on those items which are deemed most popular for the next week. Stor’d becomes valuable for both consumers and retailers.

LOG IN
Logging in is simple. Enter your username and password or sign up for an account.
LOADING PAGE
The loading barcode holds the user's attention whilst they wait.
HOME PAGE
Shows the users' bank account balance as well as their most recent transactions. The menu below allows them to easily navigate Stor'd.
RECEIPTS
Receipts are stored in order starting with the most recent. Easily swipe left or right to peruse receipts or use the button in the top left-hand corner to manually search for a receipt by date or store.
ITEM ACCOUNT
Keeps track of individual items purchased and the subsequent weekly, monthly and yearly spending. Also informs the user of where they could have bought it for less.
VIRTUAL SHOPPING LIST
The user can add items to their shopping list via previous receipts or manually adding them in. The total amount for the list will be calculated and the user will be instructed on where to shop based on the cheapest total price.
UNIVERSAL LIST
This can only be viewed by stores that can, through selecting a region, determine the most common items on users shopping lists combined, and subsequently alter prices and give discounts.
