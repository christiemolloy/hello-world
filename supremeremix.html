FALL 2017

A remix between Supreme and Chinese-Takeout.



Supreme Remix

Tools
Adobe inDesign

My task was to combine two unrelated things into a new context. I chose Supreme streetwear and Chinese take out food. It was an interesting challenge, as Supreme already uses identities to fuel the content for their merchandise, so a remix with Chinese take-out felt 100% Supreme-like. I chose to use the template of a take-out menu and catagorize the food as the different types of people who wear Supreme: “hypebeasts", “photographers” and “internet girls”, to name a few. I replaced dish names with merchandise names and changed the tone of the copy to match Supreme’s language, for example the first page reads: “Take out or eat in. We don’t care. Get on the f***king phone and order now.”
