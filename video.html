Non-Linear Video Narrative Task: 1 minute video

When asked to create a one minute video that represents me I began to think about my role as a female athlete in the community, how that empowers me, and how that power can be transferred to other aspects on my life. I collected an equal amount of YouTube videos of female athletes and females being strong in the community. I wanted to contrast females struggling in daily life to females absolutely killing it in their domain. This contrast, seen midway through the video, is successful in moving the audience.
